Our Special .

We serve the best of all dishes. Here's some of our special dishes which you would love to taste .

We present before you the classic SPECIAL DISH: "Kung thot krathiam phrik thai " is a Thai stir-fry consisting of shrimps that are fried with garlic and pepper. This one-dish is a perfect blend of savory,garlicky, and salty. ...The shrimps are often mixed with the rest of the ingredients that typically include minced garlic, fish sauce, soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, and ground white pepper before they are stir-fried with the marinating mixture.

we also serve Sadhya –Traditional vegetarian feast of Keralites is flavoursome and filling, complete with generous use of coconut milk, coconut oil, curry leaves and rice. it is specially made for Onam and other celebrations ...the sadhya is a multi course meal that includes many vegetarian curries to be eaten with rice and the final dessert course will be payasam. The multi courses aims to balance sweet, sour, spicy and tangy dishes to celebrate the multi taste buds of the palate

Bengali thali is synonymous with fish and rice. Mutton and goat meat is also a popular choice.This thali usually feature non-veg delicacies that will keep you hankering for more ...The specialities include shukto(sweet-spicy soupy mixture of vegetables),shak(leafy vegetables), chholar dal (lentil curry),begun bhaja (pan-fried brinjal),machher jhol(traditional fish curry),maach bhaja (fish fry), mutton curry,sweet-tangy chutneys(mango,tamarind, etc).The scrumptious meal is to be finished with mishti doi (sweet yogurt)and payesh(rice and milk pudding).
